## 📝 Usage tips

Auto-Tune Pro offers two main views: Auto Mode and Graph Mode.

Use Auto Mode for live correction. It identifies the key of your song and adjusts vocal pitch automatically. Select the Retune Speed to determine how fast the plugin reacts to pitch changes. A faster setting works well for the distinct robotic sound, while a slower setting sounds natural for lead vocals.

Use Graph Mode for manual editing. This mode allows you to draw pitch lines and control the correction for specific notes. Use this for detailed edits on complex vocal performances that require precision, such as vibrato or specific timing adjustments. Adjust the Humanize setting to maintain a natural vocal quality.
